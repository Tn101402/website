Mailer design · Campaign visual · Product storytelling

Role
Graphic Designer

A beauty mailer designed to feel like a curated product experience, not simply a list of promotions.

Glam Beauty uses a softer pace of information. Product imagery, seasonal messages and promotional detail are given enough space to feel premium while the essential calls-to-action remain easy to notice.
