02 — Retail communication

AEON

Retail Mailer Campaign
Campaign layout · Product communication · Promotional hierarchy

Role
Graphic Designer
Scope
Mailer design, retail communication, campaign layout, product hierarchy
Output
Complete 35-page seasonal retail mailer

A large-volume retail system designed to make products, promotions and seasonal moments easy to navigate.

The project brings different retail categories into one clear visual language. Every spread balances promotion, price, product density and campaign identity so the reader can browse quickly without losing the rhythm of the overall mailer.
